title: Sweet Potato Waffles
tags:

ingredients:
- 2/3 cup cooked, mashed sweet potato
- 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup white whole wheat flour
- 1.5 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp vanilla


steps:
- Cook sweet potato (if necessary) in the microwave for 3-4 minutes. Poke holes and put on a wet paper towel on a plate. Then using caution peel skin off after cooking. 
- Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Depending on how well you mash your sweet potato, you may have a few chunks. Thatâ€™s fine. You could also put everything in a blender or food processor for a smoother batter.
- Grease waffle maker and cook 4 waffles.
- Top as desired and serve.
